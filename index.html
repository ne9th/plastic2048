Plastic2048 was created to visualize how the use of plastic lead to the endangerment of marine life.

Plastic2048

Our oceans and marine life are choking on plastic. According to Plastic Oceans, more than 8 million tons of plastic enters our oceans every year. Studies have shown that 50% of sea turtles worldwide have consumed plastic. Many seabirds were also found dead with their stomachs full of plastic waste.

There are many ways that plastics can enter our oceans. This game only shows one of them: how plastic bags travel from our homes to the ocean. Oher ways that put many marine lives at risk include plastic litter, the use of products that contain microbeads (tiny balls of plastic), and industrial leakage.

Though, many of us are fully aware of the negative effects of plastic, we don't always think about it when we make our everyday decisions. And that is the main motivation for this project––to help draw that connection between our actions and their impacts so we can learn to be more consious about the world around us.
